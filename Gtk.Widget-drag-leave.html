Gtk.Widget::drag-leave

function callback(widget, context:Gdk.DragContext, time:Number, ):void;

The ::drag-leave signal is emitted on the drop site when the cursor leaves the widget. A typical reason to connect to this signal is to undo things done in Gtk.drag-motion, e.g. undo highlighting with Gtk.Widget.prototype.drag_unhighlight

widget

instance of Gtk.Widget that is emitting the signal

context

the drag context

time

the timestamp of the motion event
