Gtk.Revealer

const Gtk = imports.gi.Gtk;

let revealer = new Gtk.Revealer({
    reveal_child: value,
    transition_duration: value,
    transition_type: value,
});

The GtkRevealer widget is a container which animates the transition of its child from invisible to visible.

The style of transition can be controlled with Gtk.set_transition_type.

The GtkRevealer widget was added in GTK+ 3.10.
