Gtk.HeaderBar

const Gtk = imports.gi.Gtk;

let header_bar = new Gtk.HeaderBar({
    custom_title: value,
    hpadding: value,
    spacing: value,
    subtitle: value,
    title: value,
    vpadding: value,
});

GtkHeaderBar is similar to a horizontal Gtk.Box, it allows to place children at the start or the end. In addition, it allows a title to be displayed. The title will be centered with respect to the width of the box, even if the children at either side take up different amounts of space.
